EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

Security Protocols (Level 1-5)

- Always verify your ID badge before accessing secure areas.
- Level 3+ clearance required for operating rooms.
- Report any suspicious activity immediately to Security.
- Unauthorized access will result in termination.

Access Clearance Details

- Level 1: General areas
- Level 2: Maintenance and storage
- Level 3: Laboratory
- Level 4: Operating Rooms
- Level 5: Administrative & Admin Panel access

Handling of Restricted Materials

- All experimental subjects must be properly secured.
- Fusion and biohazard materials require dual authorization.
- Follow the operating room checklist before and after procedures.
- Any irregularities must be documented immediately.

Emergency Procedures

- Evacuate immediately if alarms sound in your area.
- Follow the designated escape routes posted in each corridor.
- Report to your Level Supervisor once outside.
- Do not attempt to save experiments or equipment unless explicitly ordered.
